Help Post 2 Message

  • When you sign up for Post 2 Message, you have the option to keep your Message public (the default account setting) or to protect your Message. 
  • Accounts with protected Message require manual approval of each and every person who may view that account's Message. 
  • Public Message (the default setting) are visible to anyone, whether or not they have a Post 2 Message account.
  • Protected Message may only be visible to your approved Message followers.
  • Public Tweets (the default setting) are visible to anyone, whether or not they have a Twitter account.
  • Protected Tweets may only be visible to your approved Twitter followers.
